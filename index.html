Common Questions

FAQ

What is the Muhurtham time?
The auspicious Muhurtham (Thali tying ceremony) is scheduled between 12:22 PM and 12:44 PM. Please be seated at the Kshethram before 12:00 PM.
Where is the wedding ceremony?
The wedding ceremony will take place at Thazhuthala Sree Mahaganapathy Kshethram, Thazhuthala, Kerala 691571. Please use the map link in the Details section for directions.
Where is the reception?
The reception will be held at J&J Auditorium, Mulavana, Kerala from 6:30 PM onwards. Dinner will be served at the reception venue.
Is parking available?
Yes, ample parking is available at both the Kshethram and J&J Auditorium. Volunteers will guide you to the parking area.
Can I take photos during the ceremony?
Absolutely! We encourage you to capture and share moments from the wedding. You can upload your photos directly through our Guest Gallery section on this website.
Will food be served at the ceremony venue?
Yes, a traditional Kerala Sadya (vegetarian feast) will be served at the Kshethram after the ceremony. Dinner will also be served at the reception at J&J Auditorium.
